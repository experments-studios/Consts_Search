This website **does not directly collect any personal data**. We do not use contact forms, membership systems, comment sections, or direct analytical/marketing cookies.

Third-Party Processing (Cloudflare)

However, our website is hosted and served using **Cloudflare Pages**. Cloudflare processes certain technical data automatically to ensure the security (such as protection against DDoS attacks) and performance (faster loading times) of our website. This technical data may include:

  • IP Addresses: Visitor IP addresses are processed by Cloudflare for security and traffic management purposes.
  • Security Cookies: Cloudflare may use "strictly necessary" cookies for essential website functionality and security (e.g., to detect bots). These cookies do not collect personal data or track you.
  • Traffic Logs: General, anonymized traffic data may be logged for security and performance monitoring.

For more information about Cloudflare's data processing practices, please visit Cloudflare's Privacy Policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

Scope
